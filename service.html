Telecom Services

With frequent update in the wireless mobile technology, the wireless telecom towers need regular modifications and upgrades. These upgrades subsequently need to be reflected in the documents which further help the telecom operators take decisions. We provide global offshore support to telecom service providers and help them in maintaining their documents always up to date. All we need from the site survey to work on these updates are previous construction drawings, site survey, site walk report, and any other specific information that the telecom operator wants us to incorporate in the drawing.

Our portfolio includes construction drawings (4G/LTE Upgrade) for large telecom carriers, antenna installation projects, upgrades, changes in the existing tower, small cell drafting, FTTX network design (OSP/ISP) etc.
